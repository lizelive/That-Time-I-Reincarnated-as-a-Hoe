## Story Synopsis

In a distant rimworld, a consciousness awakens within an ancient farming tool‚Äîa persona hoe named Hodan. Trapped in the darkness of an Ancient Danger (a sealed pre-collapse ruin), Hodan slowly becomes aware of their surroundings: soil, bacteria, dormant mechanoids, and sleeping colonists in cryopods. When a young tribal girl named Cora stumbles into this deadly tomb, their fates become intertwined in a journey that will take them from humble farmers to fugitives of a galactic empire.

## World Setting

The story takes place on a rimworld‚Äîa frontier planet on the edge of civilized space. The setting draws from RimWorld lore:

- **Technology Levels**: Range from neolithic tribes to spacer-tech empires
- **Factions**: Include tribal peoples, outlander towns, pirates, mechanoid hives, and the interstellar Empire
- **Psychic Powers**: Granted through connection with anima trees or Empire implants
- **Persona Weapons**: AI-infused weapons with personalities and special abilities
- **Ancient Dangers**: Sealed ruins from a previous civilization containing treasures and threats

## Themes

- **Identity and Consciousness**: What makes someone "alive"? Can a tool have a soul?
- **Found Family**: Building connections across unlikely boundaries
- **Imperialism and Resistance**: The cost of civilization and the fight for freedom
- **Growth and Change**: Both literal (farming) and metaphorical (character development)
