# Chapter 13: Giant Leaves

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Seedling |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | The Psychite Farm |
| **Key Events** | Unprecedented growth, regional attention |

## Summary

The psychite field expands beyond all expectations. Hodan's enhanced farming abilities produce plants that dwarf normal specimens—leaves the size of shields, stalks thick as arms. Word spreads through the region of the miraculous farm, bringing both opportunity and unwanted attention.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: Morning Survey
**Visual**: Cora and Hodan overlooking the expanded field at dawn.  
**Narration**: "Every morning I see them grow. Every morning I'm surprised by how much."  
**Purpose**: Establishing the routine success.

### Frame 2: Giant Plants
**Visual**: Psychite plants towering over Cora—leaves enormous, vibrant green.  
**Narration**: "These aren't normal plants anymore. They're... monuments to growth."  
**Purpose**: Visualizing the abnormal success.

### Frame 3: Village Wonder
**Visual**: Villagers gathering at the field's edge, pointing, murmuring.  
**Narration**: "'How does she do it?' they ask. They don't ask about me. They don't know."  
**Purpose**: External perception.

### Frame 4: Measurement
**Visual**: Cora measuring a leaf—it's larger than her torso.  
**Narration**: "'Hodan, this leaf could shelter three people from rain.' She's not exaggerating."  
**Purpose**: Concrete scale demonstration.

### Frame 5: Quality Testing
**Visual**: Processing some leaves, examining the dried product.  
**Narration**: "The quality matches the size. Maybe exceeds it. This tea will be extraordinary."  
**Purpose**: Product excellence confirmed.

### Frame 6: Finn's Wonder
**Visual**: Young Finn running through the field, dwarfed by plants.  
**Narration**: "'It's a forest!' he shouts. 'A green forest of tea!'"  
**Purpose**: Childlike perspective, family moment.

### Frame 7: Grandmother Willow's Visit
**Visual**: The elder examining a plant, expression thoughtful.  
**Narration**: "'The anima tree stirs when you farm,' she tells Cora. 'It notices you.'"  
**Purpose**: Foreshadowing future connection.

### Frame 8: Trader Arrival
**Visual**: Merchants approaching, eyes wide at the field.  
**Narration**: "Word travels. The first of many curious merchants arrives."  
**Purpose**: Commercial attention begins.

### Frame 9: First Major Sale
**Visual**: Cora negotiating with a well-dressed trader.  
**Narration**: "He offers triple the normal price. Cora asks for four times. She gets it."  
**Purpose**: Economic success.

### Frame 10: Growing Fame
**Visual**: Multiple traders at the field, competing for product.  
**Narration**: "One becomes three. Three becomes ten. The farm's reputation spreads."  
**Purpose**: Escalating interest.

### Frame 11: Hodan's Pride
**Visual**: Warm glow from Hodan, satisfaction visible.  
**Narration**: "I made this. We made this. From nothing, we built something extraordinary."  
**Purpose**: Emotional fulfillment.

### Frame 12: Warning Signs
**Visual**: Shadowy figures watching from the tree line.  
**Narration**: "But success has eyes. Not all of them friendly."  
**Purpose**: Foreshadowing danger.

### Frame 13: Map Spread
**Visual**: A map with the farm's location marked, passed between hands.  
**Narration**: "Somewhere, someone is drawing our location on a map. For what purpose?"  
**Purpose**: Unknown threat building.

### Frame 14: Cora's Concern
**Visual**: Cora looking worried, holding Hodan.  
**Narration**: "'We're getting too visible,' she whispers. 'Too successful.'"  
**Purpose**: Protagonist awareness of risk.

### Frame 15: Decision to Continue
**Visual**: The field at sunset, beautiful and dangerous.  
**Narration**: "But we can't stop now. The village depends on us. We'll just have to be ready."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion—success with looming threat.
