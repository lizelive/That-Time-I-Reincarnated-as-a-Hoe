# Chapter 31: Desertion

> *Arc 5: Flowering*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Flowering |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Imperial territory, wilderness |
| **Key Events** | Flight from the capital, pursuit fears, survival journey |

## Summary

Cora and Hodan flee through Imperial territory, every step taking them further from power and closer to danger. Desertion is treason; they travel in disguise, avoiding patrols, knowing that capture means death—or worse, the Soul Forge.
