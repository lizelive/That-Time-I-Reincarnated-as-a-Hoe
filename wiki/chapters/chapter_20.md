# Chapter 20: Rank and File

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |

## Summary

Cora decides to pursue Imperial citizenship, seeking the rank that will protect her village and business. Count Aldric offers sponsorship, and the arc concludes with Cora and Hodan departing for the Empire capital—leaving their simple life behind.
