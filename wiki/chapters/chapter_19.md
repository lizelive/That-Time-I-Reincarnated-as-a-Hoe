# Chapter 19: Imperial Salvation

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Seedling |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | The village, battlefield |
| **Key Events** | Empire traders intervene, raiders defeated, rank rejection |

## Summary

An Empire trade caravan arrives at the critical moment, their guards decisively routing the raiders. But salvation comes with a price—the traders refuse to deal with Cora due to her low social rank, revealing the limitations of life outside Imperial society.

**Visual**: Armed traders cresting the hill, Imperial banners flying.  
**Narration**: "Empire traders. Dozens of guards. Armed to the teeth."

**Narration**: "Ten against seventy? The math changes instantly."  
**Purpose**: Power shift.

### Frame 9: No Rank
**Visual**: Cora's confused expression.
