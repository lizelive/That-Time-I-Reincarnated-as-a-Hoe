# Chapter 35: Peace Before Storm

> *Arc 5: Flowering*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Flowering |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Cora's village |
| **Key Events** | Family time, normalcy, final peaceful moments |

## Summary

A quiet interlude showing ordinary life at its best—family meals, farming work, simple joys. This chapter deliberately establishes what will be lost, making the coming tragedy more impactful. The peace feels precious because it's temporary.
