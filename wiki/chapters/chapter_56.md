# Chapter 56: Ship at Last

> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

| Attribute | Value |

| **Arc** | Dispersal |

## Summary

After weeks of treacherous journey, Cora and Hodan finally reach Charles Whitestone's crashed starship. The vessel is damaged but viable—and Charles himself proves to be a remarkable AI with deep memories of Hodan's past life.
