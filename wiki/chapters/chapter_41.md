# Chapter 41: The Deserters

> *Arc 6: Pollination*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Pollination |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Wilderness, Deserter Base |
| **Key Events** | Finding the resistance, meeting Sergeant Vex |

## Summary

Searching for a way to fight the Empire, Cora and Hodan find the Deserters—a guerrilla resistance movement of former Imperial soldiers. Their leader, Sergeant Vex, tests Cora before accepting her. A new phase of the war begins.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: Searching
**Visual**: Cora tracking through wilderness, following rumors.  
**Narration**: "'They say there's a resistance. Former soldiers. They know how to hurt the Empire.'"  
**Purpose**: Goal established.

### Frame 2: Tracked Back
**Visual**: Shadows in the trees, they're being watched.  
**Narration**: "We've been following THEM. But they've been following US."  
**Purpose**: Reversal.

### Frame 3: Ambush
**Visual**: Armed figures emerging, weapons aimed.  
**Narration**: "'Don't move. Hands visible. Who are you?'"  
**Purpose**: Tense meeting.

### Frame 4: Explanation
**Visual**: Cora speaking carefully, Hodan hidden.  
**Narration**: "'Cora. Former Dame of the Empire. Deserter. I want to FIGHT.'"  
**Purpose**: Introduction.

### Frame 5: Skepticism
**Visual**: Resistance fighters exchanging looks.  
**Narration**: "'Dame? Noble? Here to fight? More likely here to spy.'"  
**Purpose**: Distrust.

### Frame 6: The Leader
**Visual**: Sergeant Vex stepping forward, scarred, commanding.  
**Narration**: "'I'll judge. Bring her.'"  
**Purpose**: Vex introduction.

### Frame 7: The Base
**Visual**: Hidden camp in a canyon, well-defended.  
**Narration**: "Their base. Hidden from Imperial scans. Home to maybe fifty fighters."  
**Purpose**: Setting revealed.

### Frame 8: Interrogation
**Visual**: Vex questioning Cora, intense.  
**Narration**: "'Why desert? Why now? What do you WANT?'"  
**Purpose**: Testing motivations.

### Frame 9: Cora's Truth
**Visual**: Cora's pain visible.  
**Narration**: "'They killed my mother. My grandmother. For LEAVING. I want them to pay.'"  
**Purpose**: Authentic response.

### Frame 10: Vex's Assessment
**Visual**: The sergeant studying Cora, nodding slowly.  
**Narration**: "'Pain's real. Hate's real. Question is: can you be USEFUL?'"  
**Purpose**: Pragmatic acceptance.

### Frame 11: Hodan Revealed
**Visual**: Vex noticing Hodan, eyes widening.  
**Narration**: "'That's a persona weapon. You brought IMPERIAL PROPERTY into my camp.'"  
**Purpose**: Complication.

### Frame 12: Defense
**Visual**: Cora's fierce response.  
**Narration**: "'Hodan is not PROPERTY. Hodan is a PERSON. Made through murder.'"  
**Purpose**: Moral stance.

### Frame 13: Interest
**Visual**: Vex reconsidering.  
**Narration**: "'You know about the Soul Forge?' 'I've SEEN it.'"  
**Purpose**: Connection made.

### Frame 14: Accepted
**Visual**: Vex extending a hand.  
**Narration**: "'Then you understand what we're fighting. Welcome to the Deserters.'"  
**Purpose**: Acceptance.

### Frame 15: New Purpose
**Visual**: Cora among the rebels, looking around.  
**Narration**: "A new family. A family of fighters. Time to learn their ways."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion, integration begins.
