> *Arc 5: Flowering*

| Attribute | Value |

## Summary

The attack ends with the Janissaries withdrawing—their target escaped, but the message delivered. Cora and the survivors emerge to find their world destroyed. Grief, guilt, and the first sparks of vengeance define this chapter.

**Visual**: Janissaries pulling back, mission considered complete.  
**Narration**: "'Target escaped into wilderness. Village neutralized. Report success.'"  
**Purpose**: Attack ends.

### Frame 2: Silence

**Narration**: "The silence is worse than the screaming. It means it's over."  
**Purpose**: Aftermath begins.

**Visual**: The anima tree, scarred but standing.
