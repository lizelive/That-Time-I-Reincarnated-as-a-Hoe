# Chapter 18: Crackpot's Revenge

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Summary

Ten heavily armed raiders arrive, claiming Crackpot as their "brother" and demanding compensation for his death. The village faces overwhelming force with no clear way to win. A desperate defensive stand begins.
