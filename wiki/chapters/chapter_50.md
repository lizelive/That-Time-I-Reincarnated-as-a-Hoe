# Chapter 50: Seeds of Change

> *Arc 6: Pollination*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |

| **Location** | Wilderness, Deserter Base |

## Summary

The team escapes to safety and reunites with the Deserters. The footage of Mira's humiliation spreads across the sector, inspiring other resistance cells. The Empire is weakened—but now hunting them harder than ever. The Pollination Arc concludes with seeds of rebellion planted far and wide.
