# Chapter 54: Mountain Pass

> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|

| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |

## Summary

The team traverses the deadly mountain range. Freezing temperatures, treacherous paths, and dwindling supplies test them to the limit. Not everyone will make it across—the mountains demand their toll.
