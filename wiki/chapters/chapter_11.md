# Chapter 11: New Earth

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Seedling |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Journey to Cora's village |
| **Key Events** | First journey, village arrival, introduction to family |

## Summary

Cora brings Hodan and Chitter back to her village. Hodan experiences the surface world for the first time, learns about Cora's background, and meets her family. The villagers are wary but accept the strange trio.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: The Surface
**Visual**: Bright sunlight, green trees, open sky—overwhelming after the tomb.  
**Narration**: "I thought I understood light. I was wrong. The surface world BURNS with color."  
**Purpose**: Establishing new environment.

### Frame 2: Cora Walks
**Visual**: Cora walking confidently through forest, familiar with the path.  
**Narration**: "She knows this land. Every tree, every path. She's going home."  
**Purpose**: Cora's competence and belonging.

### Frame 3: First Conversation
**Visual**: Cora speaking aloud to Hodan as they walk.  
**Narration**: "'I'm Cora,' she says. 'I was foraging when I found that... place. What are you?'"  
**Purpose**: Relationship building.

### Frame 4: Explanation
**Visual**: Hodan's glow as they communicate.  
**Narration**: "'I'm Hodan. A persona hoe. I don't fully understand what that means either.'"  
**Purpose**: Honest uncertainty.

### Frame 5: Acceptance
**Visual**: Cora shrugging, smiling slightly.  
**Narration**: "'A talking hoe. Sure. Why not. Stranger things happened today.'"  
**Purpose**: Cora's adaptability.

### Frame 6: Chitter Follows
**Visual**: Chitter scuttling alongside, investigating everything.  
**Narration**: "Chitter seems overwhelmed too. But they won't leave us. Loyal to the end."  
**Purpose**: Trio established.

### Frame 7: Village in Sight
**Visual**: Small settlement visible through trees.  
**Narration**: "Buildings. People. A community. Things I've never seen."  
**Purpose**: Destination reached.

### Frame 8: Initial Reaction
**Visual**: Villagers noticing Cora's return, then seeing Chitter.  
**Narration**: "Fear. Suspicion. 'Cora? What is THAT?'"  
**Purpose**: Normal response to unusual.

### Frame 9: Grandmother Willow Appears
**Visual**: Elderly woman approaching, calm amidst the tension.  
**Narration**: "An elder. Authority in her eyes. She's not afraid of us."  
**Purpose**: Introducing key character.

### Frame 10: Assessment
**Visual**: Grandmother Willow examining Hodan closely.  
**Narration**: "'A soul displaced. Seeking roots.' She sees me. TRULY sees me."  
**Purpose**: Willow's insight.

### Frame 11: Acceptance Granted
**Visual**: Willow nodding to the village.  
**Narration**: "'This one is welcome. And the creature. They mean no harm.'"  
**Purpose**: Authority solving conflict.

### Frame 12: Meeting the Family
**Visual**: A worried woman and a young boy running toward Cora.  
**Narration**: "'CORA!' A mother's relief. A brother's excitement."  
**Purpose**: Family introduction.

### Frame 13: Finn
**Visual**: Young boy staring at Hodan with wonder, not fear.  
**Narration**: "'Is that a magic hoe? Can it talk? CAN I HEAR IT?'"  
**Purpose**: Finn's character established.

### Frame 14: Home
**Visual**: A simple wooden house—small but warm.  
**Narration**: "Cora's home. I've never been in a home. It feels... right."  
**Purpose**: Belonging beginning.

### Frame 15: First Night
**Visual**: Quiet evening, Cora holding Hodan, family around a fire.  
**Narration**: "My first night above ground. With people. With family—not mine, but somehow... mine."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion, new beginning.
