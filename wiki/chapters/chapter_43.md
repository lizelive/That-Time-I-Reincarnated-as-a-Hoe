# Chapter 43: Strike and Fade

> *Arc 6: Pollination*

| Attribute | Value |

## Summary

Cora participates in her first rebel operation—an ambush of an Imperial supply convoy. The attack succeeds through careful planning and asymmetric tactics. Cora proves herself; Hodan discovers new capabilities in combat situations.
