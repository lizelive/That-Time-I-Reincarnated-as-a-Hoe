# Chapter 60: Among the Stars

> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Dispersal |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Starship in transit |
| **Key Events** | Epilogue, reflection, new beginnings, questions remaining |

## Summary

In the quiet of space, Cora and Hodan reflect on everything they've experienced. They have evidence to share, a ship to travel in, and each other. The story ends not with conclusions but with possibilities—seeds scattered to the stars, ready to grow in new soil.
