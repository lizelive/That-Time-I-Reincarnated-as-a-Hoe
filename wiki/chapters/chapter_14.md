# Chapter 14: The Rabbit Hunt

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Seedling |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Fields near the farm |
| **Key Events** | Encountering Count Aldric Voss, the mad hare appears |

## Summary

While tending the fields, Cora and Hodan encounter a noble in distress—Count Aldric Voss, pursued by a psychite-maddened hare. The seemingly absurd situation turns dangerous as the chemically-enhanced rabbit proves far more deadly than expected.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: Peaceful Morning
**Visual**: Cora working the field, Hodan sensing the soil, routine.  
**Narration**: "Another day. Another round of growth. Peace—"  
**Purpose**: Calm before disruption.

### Frame 2: Distant Shouts
**Visual**: Sound effects in the distance, disturbance.  
**Narration**: "Screaming. From the forest edge. Human screaming."  
**Purpose**: Inciting incident.

### Frame 3: The Runner
**Visual**: A well-dressed man sprinting from the trees, panic in his eyes.  
**Narration**: "A noble. Fine clothes torn. Bleeding from the face. Running for his life."  
**Purpose**: Aldric's introduction.

### Frame 4: Cora Reacts
**Visual**: Cora grabbing Hodan, moving to intercept.  
**Narration**: "'Help me!' he screams. 'IT'S COMING!'"  
**Purpose**: Heroic response begins.

### Frame 5: The Pursuer
**Visual**: A hare bursting from the trees—but wrong. Bulging muscles, foam, manic eyes.  
**Narration**: "A... rabbit? That's not— that's not NATURAL."  
**Purpose**: The threat revealed.

### Frame 6: Recognition
**Visual**: Hodan sensing the creature's chemistry.  
**Narration**: "Psychite. It's been eating psychite leaves. The chemicals have driven it mad."  
**Purpose**: Understanding the threat.

### Frame 7: Aldric Falls
**Visual**: The noble tripping, sprawling on the ground.  
**Narration**: "He goes down. The rabbit closes in. Teeth bared."  
**Purpose**: Immediate danger.

### Frame 8: Cora Intervenes
**Visual**: Cora swinging Hodan at the rabbit, forcing it back.  
**Narration**: "'GET BACK!' Cora swings me. The rabbit dodges—FAST."  
**Purpose**: Combat begins.

### Frame 9: Speed Surprise
**Visual**: The rabbit moving faster than expected, a blur.  
**Narration**: "It's... so fast. The psychite has enhanced everything. Speed. Aggression. Durability."  
**Purpose**: Establishing genuine threat.

### Frame 10: First Hit
**Visual**: Cora landing a glancing blow, but the rabbit recovers instantly.  
**Narration**: "I connect! But it barely notices. It's coming back."  
**Purpose**: Combat difficulty.

### Frame 11: Aldric's Plea
**Visual**: The noble crawling backward, blood dripping.  
**Narration**: "'Please, don't let it— I'll pay anything—'"  
**Purpose**: Stakes and future debt.

### Frame 12: Hodan's Analysis
**Visual**: Hodan sensing the rabbit's movements, predicting patterns.  
**Narration**: "'Left, Cora! It always dodges LEFT!' I push the knowledge through our bond."  
**Purpose**: Tactical contribution.

### Frame 13: Coordinated Strike
**Visual**: Cora adjusting, anticipating the dodge.  
**Narration**: "She trusts me. Strikes where I say. The rabbit dodges INTO the blow."  
**Purpose**: Partnership effectiveness.

### Frame 14: Cliffhanger
**Visual**: The rabbit staggered but not down, eyes more crazed than ever.  
**Narration**: "It's hurt. But not stopped. And now it's ANGRY."  
**Purpose**: To be continued.
