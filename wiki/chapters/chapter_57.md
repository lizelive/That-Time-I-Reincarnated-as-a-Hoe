> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

| Attribute | Value |

## Summary

Cora and Hodan work with Charles to repair the ship and power up the reactor. Success brings the vessel back to life—but the power signature broadcasts their location. Imperial forces and other enemies begin converging on their position.
