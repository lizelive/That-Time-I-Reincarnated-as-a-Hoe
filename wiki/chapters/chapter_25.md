# Chapter 25: The Psylink

> *Arc 4: Growth*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Growth |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Psylink Academy |
| **Key Events** | Psychic implant, new powers, something wrong |

## Summary

As reward for exceptional service, Cora receives a psylink implant—technology that grants psychic abilities. The procedure is successful, granting new powers, but Hodan senses something fundamentally wrong about the artificial enhancement.

**Visual**: Official notification, psylink eligibility.  
**Narration**: "'Noble privilege. Psylink implantation approved.' Power beyond farming."  
**Purpose**: Opportunity presented.

**Narration**: "'Psychic abilities, Hodan. Like the Janissaries. Should I?'"  
**Purpose**: Weighing decision.
