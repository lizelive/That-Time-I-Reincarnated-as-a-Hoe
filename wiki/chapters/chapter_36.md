# Chapter 36: Janissary Drums

> *Arc 5: Flowering*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Flowering |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Cora's village, perimeter |
| **Key Events** | Empire arrives, Commander Theron, ultimatum given |

## Summary

Commander Theron leads a Janissary squad to the village, announcing their purpose: capture the deserter Dame Cora and recover Imperial property (Hodan). The ultimatum is given: surrender or face destruction. The village must choose.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: Dawn Alarm
**Visual**: Village waking to shouts, Hodan's soil-sense screaming.  
**Narration**: "They're here. THEY'RE HERE."  
**Purpose**: Arrival shock.

### Frame 2: The Force
**Visual**: Janissaries in formation, weapons gleaming.  
**Narration**: "Twenty soldiers. Elite psylink warriors. Against... farmers."  
**Purpose**: Overwhelming force.

### Frame 3: The Leader
**Visual**: Commander Theron stepping forward, cold-eyed.  
**Narration**: "Their leader. I can feel his psylink—harsh, powerful, empty."  
**Purpose**: Antagonist introduction.

### Frame 4: The Demand
**Visual**: Theron's voice carrying across the village.  
**Narration**: "'Dame Cora of the Empire stands accused of desertion. Surrender or face Imperial justice.'"  
**Purpose**: Formal accusation.

### Frame 5: Property Claim
**Visual**: Theron pointing at Hodan.  
**Narration**: "'That persona weapon is Imperial property. Stolen. To be returned.'"  
**Purpose**: Hodan threatened.

### Frame 6: Village Gathers
**Visual**: Villagers emerging, scared but standing.  
**Narration**: "They're terrified. But they're not running. Not yet."  
**Purpose**: Community stance.

### Frame 7: Cora Steps Forward
**Visual**: Cora facing Theron, Hodan in hand.  
**Narration**: "'I am no longer Dame. I renounce the Empire that murders to make its weapons.'"  
**Purpose**: Defiance.

### Frame 8: Theron's Dismissal
**Visual**: Cold smile, no negotiation.  
**Narration**: "'Renunciation is not accepted. Come willingly—or the village burns.'"  
**Purpose**: Threat made.

### Frame 9: Grandmother Willow
**Visual**: The elder standing beside Cora.  
**Narration**: "'We do not give our people to murderers. This has always been our way.'"  
**Purpose**: Elder defiance.

### Frame 10: Deadline
**Visual**: Theron checking a timepiece.  
**Narration**: "'You have until sunset. Then we take what's ours.'"  
**Purpose**: Countdown begins.

### Frame 11: Withdrawal
**Visual**: Janissaries pulling back, surrounding the village.  
**Narration**: "They don't need to storm us. They just need to wait."  
**Purpose**: Siege established.

### Frame 12: Village Council
**Visual**: Urgent meeting, fear and anger.  
**Narration**: "'Give her up!' 'Never!' 'We can't fight them!' 'We can't surrender!'"  
**Purpose**: Division.

### Frame 13: Ashara's Stand
**Visual**: Mother speaking firmly.  
**Narration**: "'My daughter is not for sale. If the village wants to betray her, say so.'"  
**Purpose**: Family loyalty.

### Frame 14: Unity
**Visual**: Village choosing to stand together.  
**Narration**: "'We stand together. Whatever comes.' Fear—but solidarity."  
**Purpose**: Decision made.

**Visual**: Sun lowering, shadows lengthening.  
**Narration**: "Hours pass. The light fades. And with it, any hope of peace."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion, doom approaches.
