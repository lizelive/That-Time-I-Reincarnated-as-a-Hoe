# Chapter 24: Dame Cora

> *Arc 4: Growth*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Growth |

| **Location** | Empire Capital, Noble Court |

## Summary

Through continued exceptional service and Aldric's advocacy, Cora is elevated to Dame—the lowest noble rank but nobility nonetheless. With noble status comes power, privilege, and access to the Empire's inner workings. Cora begins to change.

**Narration**: "'I nominate Yeoman Cora for elevation. Her service merits recognition.'"
