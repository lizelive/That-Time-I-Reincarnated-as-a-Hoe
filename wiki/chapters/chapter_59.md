# Chapter 59: Liftoff

> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Dispersal |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Charles's Ship, then atmosphere |

## Summary

With enemies overwhelming their position, Charles completes the emergency repairs and initiates launch. The ship rises under fire, breaking through the attacks and climbing toward space. After everything, they escape—leaving the planet and the Empire behind.
