# Chapter 52: The Old Ship

> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Dispersal |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Communication with Charles |

## Summary

Charles shares the history of the starship and what happened when it crashed. Hodan learns more about their past life, and the scope of the escape plan becomes clear. The journey will be dangerous, but the reward—freedom—makes it worthwhile.
