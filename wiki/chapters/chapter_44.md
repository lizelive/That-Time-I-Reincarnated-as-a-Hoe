> *Arc 6: Pollination*

| Attribute | Value |

## Summary

Intelligence arrives: High Stellarch Mira, one of the Empire's most powerful leaders, will visit a frontier colony for an inspection. The Deserters see an opportunity for a strike that could humiliate the Empire and inspire widespread resistance.
