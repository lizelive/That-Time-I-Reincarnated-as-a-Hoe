# Chapter 32: Homecoming

> *Arc 5: Flowering*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Flowering |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Cora's village |

## Summary

Cora returns to her village, reuniting with her mother, brother, and the community. She must explain why she's returned—and what she learned about the Empire. A brief period of peace and healing begins, though shadows loom.
