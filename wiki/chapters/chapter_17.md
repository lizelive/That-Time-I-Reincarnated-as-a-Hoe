# Chapter 17: The Naked Raider

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Seedling |

| **Location** | The Psychite Farm storehouse |

## Summary

The first raid comes not as an army but as a single, naked, drug-addled pyromaniac. Crackpot infiltrates the storehouse and sets fire to the tea inventory, dying in his own blaze. The attack causes significant damage and sets up worse to come.
