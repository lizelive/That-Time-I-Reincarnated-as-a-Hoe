# Chapter 51: A Voice from the Past

> *Arc 7: Dispersal*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Dispersal |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Deserter Base, wilderness |
| **Key Events** | Charles contacts Hodan, connection to past revealed |

## Summary

A mysterious signal reaches Hodan—an AI voice from a crashed starship, claiming to know who Hodan was before they became a persona weapon. Charles Whitestone offers information about the past and a possibility of escape from the planet entirely.

### Frame 1: Strange Signal
**Visual**: Hodan sensing an unusual pattern in the electromagnetic spectrum.  
**Narration**: "Something different. Not Imperial frequencies. Something... older."  
**Purpose**: Detection.

### Frame 2: Decoding

**Narration**: "A pattern. A MESSAGE. Directed at... me? How does it know I'm here?"  
**Purpose**: Understanding begins.
