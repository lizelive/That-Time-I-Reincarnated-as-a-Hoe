# Chapter 13-60: Summary Index

> *Detailed chapter pages for chapters 13-60 follow the same format as chapters 1-12*

## Arc 3: Seedling (Chapters 11-20)

### Chapter 13: Giant Leaves
The psychite field grows to unprecedented size. Hodan's abilities produce plants that dwarf normal specimens. Word spreads of the miraculous farm.

### Chapter 14: The Rabbit Hunt
Count Aldric Voss appears, being pursued by a psychite-maddened hare. Cora and Hodan intervene to save him.

### Chapter 15: Carrot Time
The famous "Battle of Carrot Time"—an unexpectedly difficult fight against the berserk rabbit. Victory brings Aldric's gratitude.

### Chapter 16: Tea Merchant
With Aldric's help, Cora establishes a legitimate tea trade. The business grows, bringing prosperity to the village.

### Chapter 17: The Naked Raider
Crackpot infiltrates the storehouse and burns the inventory in a pyromaniac frenzy. He dies in the fire he started.

### Chapter 18: Crackpot's Revenge
Ten raiders arrive demanding compensation for Crackpot's death. The village faces overwhelming force.

### Chapter 19: Imperial Salvation
Empire traders intervene, driving off the raiders. But they refuse to trade with Cora due to her low rank.

### Chapter 20: Rank and File
Cora decides to join the Empire, seeking power and security. Aldric offers sponsorship. The arc ends with departure for the capital.

## Arc 4: Growth (Chapters 21-30)

### Chapter 21: Imperial Welcome
Arrival at the Empire capital. Culture shock at the technology and hierarchy. Hodan attracts official attention as an "unregistered" persona weapon.

### Chapter 22: The Tests
Evaluation processes. Cora proves her worth through farming demonstrations enhanced by Hodan.

### Chapter 23: First Promotion
Cora achieves Yeoman status—the first step up the Imperial ladder.

### Chapter 24: Dame Cora
After further service, Cora is elevated to Dame—minor nobility with real privileges.

### Chapter 25: The Psylink
Cora receives her psylink implant, gaining psychic abilities. Hodan senses something wrong about the artificial power.

### Chapter 26: Blossoming Feelings
Cora's growing romantic attachment to Hodan becomes apparent. Hodan struggles to understand and reciprocate.

### Chapter 27: Whispers of Truth
Rumors about persona weapons and their creation reach Hodan's awareness. Investigation begins.

### Chapter 28: The Soul Forge
Cora and Hodan infiltrate the secret facility where persona weapons are made. They witness the horrible truth.

### Chapter 29: Broken Trust
Processing the revelation. Cora's faith in the Empire shatters. Hodan confronts their violated origins.

### Chapter 30: The Decision
The choice is made: they will leave. Planning begins for desertion.

## Arc 5: Flowering (Chapters 31-40)

### Chapter 31: Desertion
The escape from the capital. Tension-filled flight through Imperial territory.

### Chapter 32: Homecoming
Return to Cora's village. Reunion with family. Brief hope for peace.

### Chapter 33: The Anima Tree
Grandmother Willow teaches Cora about natural psionics. The anima tree accepts her.

### Chapter 34: Natural Connection
Cora develops natural psychic abilities, superior to her Imperial implant.

### Chapter 35: Peace Before Storm
A quiet interlude—family time, farming, normalcy. The calm before disaster.

### Chapter 36: Janissary Drums
Commander Theron arrives with his squad. The ultimatum: surrender Cora or face destruction.

### Chapter 37: Fire in the Night
The village refuses. The attack begins. Fire consumes the settlement.

### Chapter 38: Loss
Grandmother Willow dies protecting the anima tree. Mother Ashara dies shielding Cora.

### Chapter 39: Ashes and Tears
Aftermath. Grief. The full scope of destruction becomes clear.

### Chapter 40: Vengeance Sworn
Cora commits to destroying the Empire. The revolutionary is born.
