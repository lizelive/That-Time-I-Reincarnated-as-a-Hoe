# Chapter 26: Blossoming Feelings

> *Arc 4: Growth*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Growth |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Empire Capital, private moments |
| **Key Events** | Romance emerges, love confessed, Hodan struggles |

## Summary

Cora's feelings for Hodan evolve beyond partnership into something like romantic love. She confesses her feelings, and Hodan must grapple with what love means for a consciousness without a human body. Their bond deepens even as questions multiply.

**Visual**: Cora in her quarters, holding Hodan, quiet reflection.  
**Narration**: "She holds me often now. Not for farming. Just to hold."

**Narration**: "'Hodan... what am I to you?'"
