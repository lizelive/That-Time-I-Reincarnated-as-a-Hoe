## Summary

The "Battle of Carrot Time"—which becomes legendary in local folklore—reaches its climax. Cora and Hodan, working in perfect synchronization, finally defeat the psychite-maddened hare in a fight that proves far more difficult than anyone would believe. Victory earns Count Aldric's eternal gratitude.

## The "Carrot Time" Legend

The battle gains its famous name through garbled retellings:
- Some claim Cora used a carrot as bait
- Others say the rabbit was protecting carrots
- The truth: no carrots were involved at all
- The name stuck because it sounds dramatic
