# Chapter 55: The Wastes

| Attribute | Value |

## Summary

From mountains to wasteland—the team enters toxic badlands where the air itself is poisonous and the ground is treacherous. Hodan's abilities become essential for navigating the hazardous terrain and finding safe paths through the contamination.
