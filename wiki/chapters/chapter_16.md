# Chapter 16: Tea Merchant

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|

| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | The Psychite Farm, trading posts |

## Summary

With Count Aldric's connections and guidance, Cora transforms the psychite farm into a legitimate business operation. The tea trade flourishes, bringing unprecedented prosperity to the village. But success brings new challenges.

**Narration**: "He returned, as promised. Not as a desperate man, but as an ally."  
**Purpose**: Follow-up on promise.
