# Chapter 22: The Tests

> *Arc 4: Growth*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Growth |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Imperial Evaluation Center |
| **Key Events** | Skills assessment, farming demonstration, proving worth |

## Summary

Cora undergoes Imperial evaluation—a series of tests designed to determine her usefulness to the Empire. Her farming abilities, enhanced by Hodan, impress the evaluators and earn her the first step toward citizenship.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: The Evaluation Center
**Visual**: Large bureaucratic building, lines of applicants.  
**Narration**: "So many seeking what we seek. Most will fail. We cannot."  
**Purpose**: Stakes established.

### Frame 2: Registration
**Visual**: Cora providing information, being recorded.  
**Narration**: "'Tribal origin. Noble sponsorship. Unusual companion.' They note everything."  
**Purpose**: Documentation.

### Frame 3: First Test—Literacy
**Visual**: Cora struggling with written examination.  
**Narration**: "She can read. Barely. Enough to pass. Just."  
**Purpose**: Showing limitations.

### Frame 4: Second Test—Physical
**Visual**: Cora running, lifting, demonstrating fitness.  
**Narration**: "Farm work built strength. She passes easily."  
**Purpose**: Natural advantages.

### Frame 5: Third Test—Skills
**Visual**: Evaluator asking about abilities.  
**Narration**: "'Farming,' Cora says. 'I'm the best farmer you've ever seen.'"  
**Purpose**: Bold claim.

### Frame 6: Skepticism
**Visual**: Evaluator's dismissive expression.  
**Narration**: "'Tribals always claim farming skills. Prove it.'"  
**Purpose**: Challenge issued.

### Frame 7: The Demonstration Field
**Visual**: A controlled growing environment, test plots.  
**Narration**: "They give us seeds. Soil. One hour. 'Impress us.'"  
**Purpose**: Test conditions.

### Frame 8: Hodan Activates
**Visual**: Cora holding Hodan, beginning to work.  
**Narration**: "Time to show them what we can do. TOGETHER."  
**Purpose**: Ability deployment.

### Frame 9: Rapid Growth
**Visual**: Plants responding to Hodan's influence, growing visibly.  
**Narration**: "The seeds sprout. Grow. In minutes, what should take days."  
**Purpose**: Demonstration of power.

### Frame 10: Evaluator Shock
**Visual**: Multiple officials gathering, staring at the results.  
**Narration**: "'That's... that's not possible.' But it IS."  
**Purpose**: Impact achieved.

### Frame 11: Questions
**Visual**: Intense interrogation about methods.  
**Narration**: "'How?' 'Trade secret,' Cora smiles. They don't like that answer."  
**Purpose**: Maintaining secrecy.

### Frame 12: Hodan Examined
**Visual**: An expert looking closely at Hodan.  
**Narration**: "'The persona weapon. It's agricultural specialized. Rare. Very rare.'"  
**Purpose**: Hodan's value recognized.

### Frame 13: Passed
**Visual**: Official stamp on documents, approval granted.  
**Narration**: "'Citizenship track approved. Skills: Exceptional. Report for assignment.'"  
**Purpose**: Success achieved.

### Frame 14: Warning
**Visual**: Evaluator's quiet words to Cora.  
**Narration**: "'That tool of yours. Keep it close. Some would kill for such a thing.'"  
**Purpose**: Danger acknowledged.

### Frame 15: First Step Taken
**Visual**: Cora leaving the center, documents in hand.  
**Narration**: "One test passed. Many more to come. But we're officially on the path."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion.
