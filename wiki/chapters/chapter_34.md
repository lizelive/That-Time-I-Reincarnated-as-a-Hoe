# Chapter 34: Natural Connection

> *Arc 5: Flowering*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Flowering |

## Summary

Cora develops her natural psychic abilities through continued connection with the anima tree. Her powers grow deeper and more stable than her psylink ever achieved. Hodan, too, feels their abilities enhanced by the tree's influence, growing more in tune with the land.
