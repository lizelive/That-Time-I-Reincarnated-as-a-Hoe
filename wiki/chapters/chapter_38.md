# Chapter 38: Loss

> *Arc 5: Flowering*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Flowering |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | The Anima Grove, village ruins |
| **Key Events** | Grandmother Willow's death, Mother Ashara's sacrifice |

## Summary

The darkest chapter. Grandmother Willow dies protecting the anima tree. Mother Ashara dies shielding Cora from a psychic blast. The two most important women in Cora's life are taken in moments. This trauma will define everything that follows.
