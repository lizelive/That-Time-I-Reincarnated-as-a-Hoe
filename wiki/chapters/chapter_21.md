# Chapter 21: Imperial Welcome

> *Arc 4: Growth*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Growth |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Empire Capital |
| **Key Events** | Capital arrival, culture shock, official attention |

## Summary

Cora and Hodan arrive at the Empire capital—a gleaming metropolis of technology and hierarchy. The overwhelming scale and sophistication creates culture shock, while Hodan attracts dangerous official interest as an "unregistered" persona weapon.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: First Sight
**Visual**: The capital city rising on the horizon, towers gleaming.  
**Narration**: "I thought the Ancient Danger was impressive. I was a fool."  
**Purpose**: Scale established.

### Frame 2: Through the Gates
**Visual**: Massive gates, guards with advanced weapons, scanners.  
**Narration**: "Scanners. They scan everyone. They scanned ME."  
**Purpose**: Security introduction.

### Frame 3: Detection
**Visual**: A guard pausing, looking at Hodan, consulting a device.  
**Narration**: "'Persona signature detected. Unregistered. Hold for processing.'"  
**Purpose**: Complication arises.

### Frame 4: Aldric Intervenes
**Visual**: Count Aldric stepping forward, presenting papers.  
**Narration**: "'My responsibility. Noble sponsorship. Check the seals.'"  
**Purpose**: Sponsor protection.

### Frame 5: Reluctant Pass
**Visual**: Guards stepping aside, suspicious but compliant.  
**Narration**: "They let us through. But they're watching now. They'll always be watching."  
**Purpose**: Ongoing surveillance.

### Frame 6: The Streets
**Visual**: Crowded streets, diverse people, overwhelming sensory input.  
**Narration**: "So many people. So many emotions. I can feel Cora's heartbeat racing."  
**Purpose**: Sensory overload.

### Frame 7: Technology Everywhere
**Visual**: Floating lights, automated systems, advanced devices.  
**Narration**: "Machines that think. Lights that float. The village seems like a dream now."  
**Purpose**: Tech contrast.

### Frame 8: Social Hierarchy
**Visual**: Nobles in fine clothes ignoring commoners, clear divisions.  
**Narration**: "The ranks are visible. You can SEE who matters and who doesn't."  
**Purpose**: Social system visualized.

### Frame 9: Aldric's Townhouse
**Visual**: A modest noble residence, still grander than anything Cora's seen.  
**Narration**: "'Guest quarters,' Aldric says. 'Rest. Tomorrow, the real work begins.'"  
**Purpose**: Temporary haven.

### Frame 10: Private Moment
**Visual**: Cora alone with Hodan, overwhelmed.  
**Narration**: "'Hodan... what have we gotten into?'"  
**Purpose**: Vulnerability.

### Frame 11: Hodan's Uncertainty
**Visual**: Hodan's glow, uncertain but supportive.  
**Narration**: "'I don't know. But we'll figure it out together.'"  
**Purpose**: Partnership affirmed.

### Frame 12: Night Visions
**Visual**: Cora looking out at the glittering city.  
**Narration**: "Somewhere out there, persona weapons are made. Made like me. From SOMEONE like me."  
**Purpose**: Foreshadowing.

### Frame 13: Chitter Hides
**Visual**: The megascarab hidden in luggage, uncomfortable.  
**Narration**: "Chitter hates the city. Too much metal. Not enough soil."  
**Purpose**: Companion's discomfort.

### Frame 14: Morning Summons
**Visual**: Official document arriving, imperial seal.  
**Narration**: "'Registration required. Persona weapon designation pending.' They want to see us."  
**Purpose**: Next challenge.

### Frame 15: Facing the Unknown
**Visual**: Cora straightening her clothing, Hodan ready.  
**Narration**: "Time to learn what we've walked into. Time to become Imperial."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion, arc beginning.
