# Chapter 23: First Promotion

> *Arc 4: Growth*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Growth |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Imperial Agricultural Corps |
| **Key Events** | Service begins, achievements, Yeoman status |

## Summary

Cora begins official Imperial service in the Agricultural Corps. Her exceptional abilities quickly earn recognition, and she achieves her first promotion to Yeoman—a commoner with citizenship rights. The climb begins.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: Assignment
**Visual**: Cora receiving orders, Agricultural Corps insignia.  
**Narration**: "'Agricultural Corps. Report to Sector 7 farms.' We have a place now."  
**Purpose**: Service begins.

### Frame 2: The Corps
**Visual**: Large-scale farming operations, mechanized but still needing workers.  
**Narration**: "Imperial farming is different. Bigger. More organized. Less... alive."  
**Purpose**: Contrast with village.

**Purpose**: Problem identified.

**Visual**: Cora implementing rotation, amendments, Hodan enhancing.  
**Narration**: "Traditional knowledge plus... me. The soil responds."  
**Purpose**: Skills applied.

### Frame 7: Rapid Results
**Visual**: Crops recovering, growing strong.  
**Narration**: "Two weeks. Yields up 50%. They notice."  
**Purpose**: Success demonstrated.

### Frame 8: Supervisor's Interest
**Visual**: An officer reviewing Cora's sector, impressed.  
**Narration**: "'How did you— the reports said this was unrecoverable.'"  
**Purpose**: Recognition begins.

### Frame 9: Credit Given

**Purpose**: Official acknowledgment.

### Frame 10: Jealousy

### Frame 11: Keeping Secret
