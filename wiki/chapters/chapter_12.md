# Chapter 12: Psychite Dreams

> *Arc 3: Seedling*

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Arc** | Seedling |
| **Perspective** | Hodan (first-person) |
| **Location** | Cora's village, surrounding land |
| **Key Events** | Beginning the psychite farm, discovering commercial potential |

## Summary

Seeking a way to help the village, Cora and Hodan begin cultivating psychite plants. Hodan's abilities make the farm extraordinarily productive, and they discover the potential for psychite tea commerce.

## Frame-by-Frame Description

### Frame 1: Village Life
**Visual**: Days passing, Cora and Hodan adapting to routine.  
**Narration**: "Village life is... slow. Peaceful. But Cora is restless."  
**Purpose**: Establishing status quo.

### Frame 2: The Problem
**Visual**: Cora looking at thin food stores.  
**Narration**: "'We need more,' she tells me. 'The village is struggling.'"  
**Purpose**: Motivation for action.

### Frame 3: Hodan's Suggestion
**Visual**: Hodan's perception of the surrounding land—fertile soil.  
**Narration**: "'Let me help. I can feel the soil. I can help things grow.'"  
**Purpose**: Offering capability.

### Frame 4: Psychite Seeds
**Visual**: Small packet of seeds—acquired from a trader.  
**Narration**: "'Psychite,' Cora explains. 'Valuable. Dangerous if misused. But we can make tea.'"  
**Purpose**: Introducing the crop.

### Frame 5: Planting
**Visual**: Cora digging, Hodan guiding the soil around seeds.  
**Narration**: "Together, we plant. I encourage the soil to accept. To nourish."  
**Purpose**: Teamwork established.

### Frame 6: Rapid Growth
**Visual**: Time-lapse of seedlings emerging faster than normal.  
**Narration**: "They respond to me. Eager. Hungry for growth."  
**Purpose**: Hodan's enhancement shown.

### Frame 7: Village Notice
**Visual**: Other villagers gathering to watch the unusual growth.  
**Narration**: "'How is it growing so fast?' they ask. Cora just smiles."  
**Purpose**: Community attention.

### Frame 8: The Secret
**Visual**: Hodan glowing slightly, connection visible.  
**Narration**: "They don't know it's me. We keep that secret. Most aren't ready for a talking hoe."  
**Purpose**: Social navigation.

### Frame 9: First Harvest
**Visual**: Cora gathering leaves, large and vibrant.  
**Narration**: "The first harvest. Enough to process. To dry. To become tea."  
**Purpose**: Achievement milestone.

### Frame 10: Tea Making
**Visual**: Traditional processing—drying, preparing.  
**Narration**: "'This is how Grandmother Willow taught me,' Cora explains."  
**Purpose**: Cultural connection.

### Frame 11: First Cup
**Visual**: Cora drinking the tea, Hodan sensing her reaction.  
**Narration**: "She drinks. Relaxation. Focus. 'It's GOOD,' she says. 'Really good.'"  
**Purpose**: Product validation.

### Frame 12: The Dream
**Visual**: Cora looking at the field with growing ambition.  
**Narration**: "'We could sell this. Not the dangerous drugs—just tea. Help the village.'"  
**Purpose**: Commercial vision forming.

### Frame 13: Grandmother Willow's Blessing
**Visual**: Elder inspecting the field, nodding approvingly.  
**Narration**: "'Use it wisely,' she says. 'Tea heals. Flake destroys. Choose healing.'"  
**Purpose**: Moral guidance.

### Frame 14: Expansion Plans
**Visual**: Cora and Hodan surveying empty land nearby.  
**Narration**: "We plan. We dream. A bigger field. More tea. A better future."  
**Purpose**: Chapter conclusion, ambition established.
