# Characters

> *"Every person on the rim has a story. Most of them end in tragedy. The interesting ones? They write their own endings."*

This section contains detailed profiles of all major and supporting characters in **The Time I Reincarnated as a Hoe**.

---

## Main Characters

### [Howen (The Hoe)](./howen.md)
**Role:** Protagonist, Persona Weapon  
**Status:** Active (as of Arc 5)

Once a human researcher, now a consciousness trapped within a persona hoe. Howen retains her intelligence, wit, and determination despite her unconventional form. After connecting with the anima trees, she becomes something the Empire never imagined—a being of unprecedented psychic power.

*"I used to have hands. Now I have purpose."*

---

### [Cora Barleybloom](./cora.md)
**Role:** Wielder, Partner, Found Family  
**Status:** Active (as of Arc 5)

A young farmer separated from her ancestral tribe as a baby. Cora's curiosity led her to discover Howen, and their bond grew from necessity to unbreakable partnership. When she finally found her family—the Barleybloom Tribe—the Empire burned them. Now she fights for vengeance.

*"I found my family twice. The Empire burned them both times. Now I'm going to burn them back."*

## Character Development Notes

### Howen's Evolution
- **Arc 1-2**: Confusion, survival instinct, discovering identity
- **Arc 3**: Building confidence, forming bonds, learning the world
- **Arc 4**: Moral complexity, weighing loyalty against justice
- **Arc 5**: Leadership, sacrifice, acceptance of purpose

### Cora's Evolution
- **Arc 2**: Fear, curiosity, desperate survival
- **Arc 3**: Growing competence, trust in Howen
- **Arc 4**: Confronting trauma, anger at the Empire
- **Arc 5**: Becoming a warrior, finding closure
