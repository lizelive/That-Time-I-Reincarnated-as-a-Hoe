# Story Arcs

The narrative of "That Time I Reincarnated as a Hoe" unfolds across seven major arcs, each named after a stage in plant development—reflecting both the farming theme and the growth of our protagonists.

## Thematic Progression

### Act I: Roots (Arcs 1-2)
The opening act establishes Hodan's unique existence and their bond with Cora. Themes of **awakening**, **survival**, and **connection** dominate as our protagonists escape certain death and begin their journey together.

### Act II: Cultivation (Arcs 3-4)
The middle act sees Cora and Hodan building a life, gaining power, and rising through Imperial society. Themes of **ambition**, **belonging**, and **forbidden knowledge** create tension as the protagonists discover dark truths about persona weapons.

### Act III: Harvest (Arcs 5-7)
The final act deals with consequences, loss, and ultimately transcendence. Themes of **rebellion**, **sacrifice**, and **freedom** drive the narrative to its cosmic conclusion.
