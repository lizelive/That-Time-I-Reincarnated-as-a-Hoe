# Locations

The world of "That Time I Reincarnated as a Hoe" spans from ancient underground ruins to the gleaming spires of the Empire capital. Each location carries its own history, dangers, and secrets.

## World Overview

The story takes place on an unnamed rimworld—a frontier planet in the outer reaches of civilized space. The planet features diverse biomes, ancient ruins, and a mix of technological eras existing simultaneously.

## Location Categories

### By Danger Level
- **Safe**: Cora's Village (usually), The Psychite Farm, Deserter Base
- **Moderate**: Empire Capital, The Anima Grove
- **Dangerous**: The Mountain Pass, Charles's Ship (during assault)
- **Extreme**: The Ancient Danger, The Soul Forge, The Toxic Wastes

### By Technology Level
- **Neolithic/Tribal**: Cora's Village, The Anima Grove
- **Industrial**: The Psychite Farm
- **Spacer**: Empire Capital, The Soul Forge
- **Glitterworld/Ultra**: The Ancient Danger, Charles's Ship

### By Faction Control
- **Tribal**: Cora's Village, The Anima Grove
- **Empire**: Empire Capital, The Soul Forge
- **Neutral/Contested**: The Psychite Farm
- **Independent**: The Ancient Danger, Charles's Ship
- **Rebel**: Deserter Base
