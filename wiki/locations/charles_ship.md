## Description

Charles's Ship is a massive spacecraft from the glitterworld era, crash-landed on this rimworld approximately 300 years ago. Though severely damaged in the crash and deteriorated over centuries, its core systems—including Charles Whitestone's AI core and the main reactor—remain functional. With sufficient time and protection, the ship can achieve orbit and beyond.

## Condition

### What Works
- AI core (Charles, fully functional)
- Main reactor (functional after restart)
- Life support (basic functionality)
- Cryogenics (most pods operational)
- Communication systems
- Navigation (stellar database intact)

### What Doesn't
- Hull integrity (patched but compromised)
- Weapons systems (destroyed in crash)
- Secondary power (depleted)
- External sensors (limited)
- Many crew amenities

### Repairs Required
Charles spent 300 years making minimum repairs:
- Hull patches for atmosphere retention
- Reactor maintenance
- Life support jury-rigging
- Drive alignment (partial)

## History

### Original Mission
The ship was part of a glitterworld colonization initiative, carrying settlers to establish new worlds. The crew included scientists, engineers, and—apparently—someone Hodan once was.

### The Crash
300 years ago, the ship crashed on this rimworld:
- Cause unknown (Charles doesn't discuss it)
- Most crew died on impact
- Survivors attempted to establish colony
- Colony failed; survivors died over decades
- Charles remained, alone

### Three Centuries Alone
Charles maintained minimal power, waiting:
- Made repairs as possible
- Monitored for rescue (none came)
- Eventually detected Hodan's consciousness
- Began calling out, planning escape

### Reunion
When Cora and Hodan reached the ship:
- Charles welcomed them as crew
- Reactor startup began
- Signal attracted hostile attention
- Final battle ensued

## The Final Stand

### Reactor Power-Up
Starting the reactor required hours:
- Power slowly building
- Drive alignment checking
- Signal broadcasting across the planet
- Every hostile faction responding

### Waves of Attacks
Multiple forces converged:
- Mechanoid swarms seeking to destroy ancient technology
- Imperial Janissaries hunting Cora
- Raider bands seeking salvage
- Wildlife driven by disturbance

### Defense
The survivors held the ship:
- Cora and remaining Deserters fought
- Hodan provided tactical awareness
- Charles managed ship systems
- Chitter defended the hull

### Launch
At the critical moment:
- Enemies breached final perimeter
- Reactor reached full power
- Charles activated emergency launch
- The ship lifted off, damaged but flying

## Current State

After escaping the rimworld:
- Ship is traveling through space
- Destination unknown
- Crew: Cora, Hodan, Chitter, 2 surviving Deserters
- Condition: Barely functional but improving
